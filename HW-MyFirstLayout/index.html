About Me

My name is Mallory and I was born and raised in Austin, Texas. I majored
in Psychology at Sam Houston State University. My favorite subject
is cognitive psychology with a focus on mental processes such as attention,
memory, creativity, and thinking. It was in this class that I discovered
Human Factors Psychology, some of which examines human behavior in
order to find the best way to design products. But it wasn't until I worked
at Indeed, recruiting for tech talent, that I realized I possessed a similar
passion for UX/UI.
